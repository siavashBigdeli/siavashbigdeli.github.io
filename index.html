Siavash Bigdeli

I am a senior data scientist in CSEM. Before that, I was a postdoctoral researcher in the IVRL group of EPFL, headed by Professor Sabine Süsstrunk. I obtained my Ph.D. at the University of Bern under the supervision of Professor Matthias Zwicker.

I am interested in the explainability of machine learning and AI. My work is mainly in Computer Vision and Graphics, specifically on machine learning techniques for image processing tasks.
